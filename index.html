Remember The Titans

Remember the Titans is a American biographical sports comedy-drama film produced by Jerry Bruckheimer and directed by Boaz Yakin.
The screenplay, written by Gregory Allen Howard, is loosely based on the true story of coach Herman Boone, portrayed by Denzel Washington,
and his attempt to integrate the T. C. Williams High School (now Alexandria City High School) football team in Alexandria, Virginia, in 1971.
Will Patton portrays Bill Yoast, Boone's assistant coach. Real-life athletes Gerry Bertier and Julius
Campbell are portrayed by Ryan Hurst and Wood Harris, respectively.
